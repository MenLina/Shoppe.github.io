Shoppe - stylish and desirable jewelry

Shoppe is a jewelry house and a popular brand of handcrafted jewelry. The company was founded in 1994 in London. Its creator is Barbara Wilson. Initially, it was a small shop that sold jewelry brought from Thailand. Later, the business expanded, the vector of activity changed, and such a brand as Shoppe appeared on the market. The product line includes bracelets, earrings and other types of jewelry.

Although Shoppe first appeared in Denmark, its main market is in the US. The brand is the third largest in this country after Signet Jewelers and Tiffany & Co, and takes a leading position in terms of site traffic.
